About Team Water

Team Water is a memecoin project created to support charitable water initiatives around the world. We are inspired by content creators and philanthropists who work to bring clean water access to communities in need. Our goal is to generate funds through our token that can contribute to these important causes.

Tokenomics Overview

Launch Strategy

The team will start with no tokens. All tokens will be distributed fairly to the community.

Token Distribution

100% of tokens go to the community. No team allocation or private sales.

Security

All wallet keys are managed safely and transparently for community protection.

Community First

This project supports water access initiatives and charitable causes that help communities worldwide.

Fee Generation System

How We Make Money

We earn fees using Bonk's fee system. This means we get free token supply without any upfront costs, which helps fund our water projects.

Free Supply Generation

Through Bonk's fee mechanism, we receive tokens at no cost. This free supply allows us to support charitable causes without requiring initial investment.

Open Records

All transactions are recorded on the blockchain. Anyone can check our activities and verify what we do.
